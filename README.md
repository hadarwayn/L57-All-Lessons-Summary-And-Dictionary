Every single entry in this dictionary is built to professional engineering standards and includes:
1. **Title & Category:** Grouped by technical domain (e.g., *Computer Vision*, *System Design*).
2. **Hebrew Translation:** Localized terminology for Israeli tech professionals.
3. **Definition:** A clear, deep, and highly accurate explanation of the concept.
4. **Synonyms & Related Items:** Cross-references to map out the AI ecosystem.
5. **Application in Industry:** Real-world examples of how top tech companies use this exact concept today.
6. **Visuals & Mathematics:** Either a clean **Mermaid Flowchart** explaining the architecture, or the **LaTeX Mathematical Formula** defining the algorithm.
7. **Recommended Video:** A curated, algorithmically-selected professional **YouTube Tutorial** link embedded at the bottom of every item!
